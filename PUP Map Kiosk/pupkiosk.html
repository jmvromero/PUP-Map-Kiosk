PUP Gymnasium




The University Gymnasium is located near Tennis court. The University Gymnasium serves as the training ground for the student athletes that participate in the intercollegiate sports program.


Interfaith Chapel




The Interfaith Chapel for students and faculty members of different faiths. It is near Nutrition and Food Science Building and East Wing.


Freedom Park




Freedom Park is a 1.5-hectare open space located in the heart of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP) campus.


Ninoy Aquino Library and Learning Resources Center




NALLRC is the main library of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. It is a three-storey building that houses a collection of over 100,000 books, journals, and other materials.


Track and Football Oval




This oval is a 100-meter track and field oval located in the PUP campus. It is used for a variety of sports activities, including track and field, football, and rugby. The oval is also a popular spot for students to relax and unwind.


Lagoon




It is a popular spot for students to relax, hanging out with friends, and eat. The lagoon is also home to a variety of food stalls, plants, and people.


Amphitheater




This is an open-air theater located in the PUP campus. It is used for a variety of events, including concerts, plays, and rallies. The amphitheater is also a popular spot for students to relax and unwind.


Linear Park




This park is a long, narrow park located along the Pasig River. It is a popular spot for students to relax, walk, and bike.


Pasig River




This is the river that flows through the heart of Metro Manila. It is also a popular spot for students to relax and unwind. The Pasig River is also home to a variety of plants and animals.


Charlie del Rosario Hall




Charlie del Rosario Hall is a multi-purpose hall located in the PUP campus. It is used for a variety of events.


Nutrition and Food Science




Nutrition and Food Science is a 4-storey building located at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP) Sta. Mesa campus. It houses the College of Nutrition and Food Science (CNFS).


Swimming Pool




Swimming Pool It is an Olympic-sized swimming pool that is capable of handling several swimming classes and student athlete activities simultaneously.


Outdoor Basketball and Volleyball Court




Outdoor gym fit for basketball, tennis, and other Palarong Pambansa.
